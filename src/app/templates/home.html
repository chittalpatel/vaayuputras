Predicting Wind Power

Power Forecasts for Wind Energy

Wind energy plays an integral role in the supply of energy world-wide. The energy output of a wind farm is highly dependent on the wind conditions present at its site. So, We aim to forecast the Power output of a wind farm from the given forecast wind conditions and Provide charts and plots for better understanding and analytics. You can collaborate with us to get personalized accurate predictions for your Wind Farm Site.

Deep Learning Solution

We have gathered a large amount of Data of around 17+ Million rows to be used for training our LSTM Model. We constantly aim to get more and more data to feed to our model to improve the model prediction. LSTM Model with Wind Speed as feature is used as it comes out to be the most co-related to the Wind Turbine Power output. Other features like Wind Direction, Temperature, Pressure are less co-related and So only Wind Speed feature is taken into account for Moddel training and deployment to give Fast and Accurate Predictions.
